  2. (A×B)⋅(C×D)=(A⋅C)(B⋅D)−(A⋅D)(B⋅C)(A\times B)\cdot(C\times D)=(A\cdot C)(B\cdot D)-(A\cdot D)(B\cdot C)(A×B)⋅(C×D)=(A⋅C)(B⋅D)−(A⋅D)(B⋅C)
